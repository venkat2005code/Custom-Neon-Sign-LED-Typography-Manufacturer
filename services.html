Frequently Asked Questions

How long does manufacturing take?

Standard production takes 3-5 business days. Shipping usually takes an additional 3-5 days depending on your location.

Are the signs safe for a child's bedroom?

Yes! Our LED neon is completely shatterproof, cool to the touch, and contains no toxic gases like traditional glass neon.

Can I use my sign outdoors?

We offer weatherproofing upgrades for exterior use. Please mention this requirement when requesting a custom quote.
