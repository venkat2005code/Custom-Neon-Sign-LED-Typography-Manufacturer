Our Story

Crafting light into art since 2020. We are passionate about creating premium, custom LED neon signs that elevate any space.

Illuminating Your Vision

At Glow, we believe that lighting is more than just illumination—it's an expression of identity, mood, and art. Founded in a small workshop by a team of passionate designers and engineers, we set out to modernize traditional neon.

We replaced fragile, energy-heavy glass tubes with durable, eco-friendly LED technology without sacrificing the mesmerizing glow that makes neon so iconic.

Today, we proudly manufacture premium custom typography for storefronts, weddings, stream setups, and home interiors across the globe.

The Glow Guarantee

Our Journey

2020

Our Humble Beginnings

Founded in a small workspace with a simple mission: to democratize custom neon. We spent our first year perfecting the LED flex tubing to ensure it was exceptionally bright, highly durable, and eco-friendly.

LED Flex R&D Eco-friendly Materials
2022

Going Global

Launched our interactive neon builder to the public, allowing anyone to design their own sign in real-time. We expanded our shipping to over 50 countries and opened our second manufacturing hub to keep up with demand.

50+ Countries New Manufacturing Hub
2024

Industry Leaders

Shipped our 10,000th custom neon sign. We partnered with major global brands to illuminate their storefronts and launched new, innovative product lines like UV-printed acrylic backgrounds.
